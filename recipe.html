Banana Bread

Banana Bread is a bread/dessert originating from North America. It was used as a way to not waste overripened bananas in the Great Depression. It was the first ever think I have baked and it colds a special place in my heart, next to carrot cake.

Ingredients

  • Overripe Bananas
  • 1/3 Cup Melted Butter
  • Baking Soda
  • White Grandulated Sugar (Or Brown Sugar)
  • 1 Egg
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • 1 Cup FLour
  • Half Cup Chocolate Chip

    • Instructions

      1. Grab large bowl 2. add bananas and mash up 3. melt 1/3 butter and combine 4. Add Baking Soda, Sugar and Egg 5. Add 1 Cup FLour 6. Mix till Smooth 7. Add Half Cup of Chicolate Chips, Fold in 8. Use pan, cover all sides with Butter 9. Add Mixture in pan, Top with more Chocolate Chips 10. Pre-Heat Oven, put pan inside for 35 Minutes 11. Leave to Cool
